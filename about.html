about

This website was initially built for my girlfriend in 2024. She used to jot down her weekly tasks using mediums such as Word and random scraps of paper. I thought it would be nice to build a cutesy, simple site so she could keep all her plans in one place.

After pushing it to the web, I realised that I used the site as much as she does! Something I always struggled with was planning meals so I thought a nice addition to the weekly planner would be a meal plan as well.

Note:
This site operates on saving your input to local storage so it won't work across multiple devices.
